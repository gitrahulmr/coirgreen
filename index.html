FAQ's

Q2: What is the scope of our service and operating location?
Our primary focus is on Alleppey and the surrounding backwaters, where we offer houseboats, shikkara rides, and authentic village experiences. We are also expanding operations across Kerala including destinations like Munnar, Thekkady, Varkala, Kovalam, Wayanad, and Kochi. Whether it's hill stations, beaches, or heritage trails, we curate every journey with care.

Q3: What is our motto?
Our motto is simple: *"Show the unseen, feel the real."* We want our guests to explore the untouched, lesser-known gems of Kerala and create memories by experiencing authentic local life, cuisine, traditions, and natural beauty — not just ticking off tourist spots.

Q4: How do we operate?
We collaborate with a handpicked and verified network of hotels, homestays, resorts, houseboats, guides, and cab drivers. All our partners are authorized, trained, and experienced to maintain safety, hygiene, and service quality. You get a personalized travel experience with 24/7 guest support during your trip.

Q5: How are we different from others?
At CoirGreen Holidays, we don’t just plan trips — we craft meaningful journeys. Unlike mass tour operators, we focus on customized service, real-time support, and immersive experiences. Most of our guests return for a second and third trip — and they refer their friends too. We believe that’s the best proof of our difference.

Q6: What payment methods do we accept?
We offer flexible and secure payment options including UPI, Google Pay, PhonePe, Bank Transfer (IMPS/NEFT), major Credit/Debit cards, and select Pay-on-Arrival options. All bookings are supported with transparent invoices and confirmations.

Q7: Can I customize my travel package?
Absolutely! We specialize in tailoring packages to your needs — whether you’re a solo traveler, couple, family, or group. From budget stays to luxury resorts, and from leisure trips to cultural explorations, everything can be adjusted based on your preferences and budget.

Q8: Is CoirGreen Holidays a safe option for solo Traveler or senior citizen travelers?
Yes. Safety is our top priority. We ensure all stays, cabs, and houseboats are verified and secure. Many of our guests include solo Travelers and elderly travelers who have had wonderful, worry-free experiences with us. We offer personalized care and can even assign local escorts or coordinators if needed.
